Gith

Gith Traits

Your character shares the following traits with other gith.

Ability Score Increase Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

Age Gith reach adulthood in their late teens and live for about a century.

Size Gith are taller and leaner than humans, with most a slender 6 ft in height.

Speed Your base walking speed is 30 ft.

Languages You can speak, read, and write Common and Gith.

Subraces There are two kinds of gith, githyanki and githzerai. Choose one of these subraces.
