Welcome Adventurers!

This database was created to help me create as many RNG (random number generator) characters as I want.

It's not that I'm preparing for my character(s) to die, I just really like making a random character every once in a while.

All information is based on the current 5th edition of The Wizard's Coast Dungeons and Dragons books. These books are: Player's Handbook, Volo's Guide to Monsters, Elemental Evil Player's Companion, and Xanthar's Guide to Everything.
